My research

College

The following research was carried out through my time at college as a student.

Big Data

Some of the aspects I researched included:

  • The 6 v's of big data
  • The hardware and software requirements for big data
  • The impact security has on big data

I also investigated different case studies about organisations using big data and the impact the use had on the organisation.

Cyber Security

Some of the aspects I researched included:

  • The GDPR
  • The Computer Misuse Act
  • Internal and external threats
  • How a cyber-attack can impact a company/organisations financial loss, reputational loss and operational loss
  • The physical and software measures a company/organisation can take to protect themselves from an internal or external threat

Networking and Communications

Some of the aspects I researched included:

  • Different types of networks (WAN, LAN, MAN etc.)
  • Different types of network cabling (co-axial, twisted pair, fibre etc.)
  • Applications used for networking
  • Network topologies (bus, ring, star etc.)
  • Network protocols (TCP/IP, FTP etc.)
  • Network models (7-layer OSI model, 5 layer OSI model)
  • Data transmission (Synchronous/Asynchronous etc.)

The Internet of Things

Some of the aspects I researched included:

  • How the Internet of Things improves devices designed for security, productivity and efficiency
  • How the Internet of Things is misused
  • How the Internet of Things is used in different sectors (health, industrial etc.)
